## Taking Screenshots

### Desktop Application
1. Open the application in your browser
2. Generate a frame with your desired settings
3. Use your operating system's screenshot tool:
   - **Windows**: Windows Key + Shift + S
   - **macOS**: Cmd + Shift + 4
   - **Linux**: PrtScn or use Screenshot tool

### Mobile AR
1. Open the AR view on your mobile device
2. Position the frame in your space
3. Take a screenshot:
   - **iOS**: Side button + Volume Up
   - **Android**: Power + Volume Down

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG (for photographs) or PNG (for UI screenshots)
- **Resolution**: At least 1920x1080 for desktop, native resolution for mobile
- **Quality**: High quality, well-lit, clear focus
- **Naming**: Use descriptive, lowercase names with underscores
